# Liberland Citizens and E-Residents

For those of you that have already completed the journey and become an E-Resident or citizen of Liberland, we congratulate you! In this area you will find information on voting, the Liberland Senate, how to claim residency, becoming a Congressperson, and more.

## Where to Use Liberland Identity Documents

Below is a comprehensive list, as of 10 November 2025, of where you can use your Liberland identity documents (E-Resident card or passport) to verify your identity and access services available to Liberlanders.

### Centralized Exchanges

- Bifinance
- Biconomy

### Financial Services

- Bitwage

### Other

- Hotels when asked for proof of identity
